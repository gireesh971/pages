## Itinerary of Kuari Pass Trek

### 14/03/26
- We pick you up from your stay in Rishikesh between 8 am to 8.30 am
- Overnight stay in Joshimath
- **Road distance:** 220 kms
- **Travel time:** 8 to 9 hours
- Stay at Joshimath will be in a hotel on double sharing basis
- Evening tea with snack and dinner at Joshimath
- **Altitude:** 6200 feet

### 15/03/26
- Post breakfast trek to Gulling Top via Tugasi/Dhak village
- Drive from Joshimath to Tugasi village: 45 mins
- **Trek distance:** Approx 4kms
- **Trek time:** Approx 3 hours
- Packed lunch will be given today
- Soup, tea with snack and dinner with a sweet will be served at the campsite at Gulling top
- Night stay in tents at Gulling top
- **Altitude:** approx 9600 feet

### 16/03/26
- Post breakfast trek to Khullara starts
- Packed lunch will be provided today
- **Trek distance:** Approx 6kms
- **Trek time:** 5 to 6 hours
- The trail is mostly steep, upward climb on a rocky trail
- Soup, tea with snacks and dinner with a sweet will be served at the campsite
- Night stay at Khullara
- **Altitude:** 11,200 feet approx

### 17/03/26
- Post breakfast trek from Khullara to Kuari Pass via Khullara top and back to Khullara campsite
- **Total trek distance:** 10 kms approx
- **Time:** 8 to 9 hours
- Steep, strenuous path
- Today you will be given packed lunch
- Soup, tea with snack and dinner with a sweet will be served at the campsite
- **Altitude of Kuari Pass:** 12500 feet approx

### 18/03/26
- This is the buffer day that can be used to stay back at Khullara campsite **OR**
- If the entire team is physically fit, this day can be used to push to Pangarchulla peak
- **Total trek distance:** 12 kms approx (round trip)
- **Time:** 10 to 12 hours
- Summit push may start at 2 am
- The trail is moderately challenging, It involves both steep ascents and descents, as well as sections with snow and ice. Trekkers should have a reasonable level of fitness and some prior trekking experience to be on this trail
- Lunch may be packed today
- Soup, tea with snack and dinner with a sweet will be served at the campsite
- **Altitude:** 15,069 feet

### 19/03/26
- Post breakfast trek down to Dhak village
- **Trek distance:** 10 kms approx
- **Trek time:** 5 to 6 hours
- Dhak to Joshimath in Tempo Traveller
- Overnight stay at Joshimath

### 20/03/26
- Breakfast at Joshimath
- Drop at Rishikesh
